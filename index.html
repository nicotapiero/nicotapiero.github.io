Recent Poetry

Digital Ghosts

Pixels dance in cathode ray dreams,
While modems sing their 56k hymns.
We are the first generation
To love through fiber optic streams,
To find ourselves in HTML.

Connection is the new religion,
Bandwidth our daily bread.
